Who We Are

We Are
Errata.

Errata is more than a choir. We offer community, snacks, musical development workshops, vocal coaching and snacks.

We are singers, snack-lovers and we are defiantly, joyfully queer. Founded in London, we believe singing is political, communal, and transformative. We perform everything from original compositions to reimagined classics, always through a lens that centres queerness, radical care, and collective joy.

No auditions. No experience required. Just your voice, exactly as it is.

Snacks. Did we mention snacks?.

—Members & Growing

Our Commitments

Equality,
Diversity & Inclusion

Errata is a choir where every queer voice belongs — no exceptions, no asterisks, no small print.

01

Radical Welcome

We centre the experiences of trans, non-binary, BIPOC, disabled, and otherwise marginalised queer people. Our space is built for those who've felt least welcome elsewhere.

02

No Auditions. Ever.

Your voice does not need to be trained, polished, or "good enough". It just needs to be yours. We welcome all voice types and ranges without exception.

03

Access & Affordability

Membership is kept as affordable as possible. Rehearsal spaces are chosen with physical accessibility in mind.

04

Safer Space Policy

Clear safer space policy, a dedicated wellbeing contact, and zero tolerance for transphobia, racism, ableism, and all forms of harm.

05

Queer Music First

Our repertoire centres music by queer, trans, and BIPOC composers and songwriters. We commission new original work that reflects who we are.

06

Accountable & Evolving

We take feedback seriously, hold regular EDI conversations, and hold ourselves accountable. We are committed to continuous improvement.

Questions about our EDI commitments? Reach out directly — we welcome honest conversations.
